User Story: As a student, I want to easily record if my meal is vegetarian or vegan and which dining hall it was in such that I can track my meals for goals and competitions.

To record a meal:

Go to the home page of the app.
Tap on Eat.
To change the type of meal you wish to record, tap Vegetarian, Vegan, or Neither.
To change the location of the meal you wish to record, tap the drop down and select either Commons, Knollcrest, Home, or Other.
